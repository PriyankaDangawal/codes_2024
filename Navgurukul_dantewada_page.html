Welcome in NavGurukul Dantewada Campus

NavGurukul Dantewada Campus opened its doors on August 1, 2023, in Dantewada, Chhattisgarh. This initiative is a collaboration between the government and Navgurukul. Our program is open to both boys and girls, making it the first co-ed campus of Navgurukul. As of March 24, we are proud to have a total of 100 students, with 60 girls and 40 boys enrolled.

Our curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive learning experiences. Students have completed various modules, including mathematics, the number system, and flowchart for building logic. Currently, they are immersed in learning their first programming language, JavaScript.

At our campus, we prioritize student development in academics, culture, and English proficiency

  • Academics: We offer a guided curriculum with full support and assessment to ensure students' academic success. Our program emphasizes self-learning, allowing students to master concepts independently.
  • English Proficiency: Creating an environment conducive to English fluency is integral to our culture. We engage students in various fun and interactive activities to promote English language skills.
  • Culture: Our campus fosters a vibrant cultural atmosphere. We organize monthly sports days, promoting physical fitness with morning exercises and evening games. Additionally, we celebrate festivals together, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among students.

We believe in providing a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and culturally. Join us on this exciting journey of learning and growth at Navgurukul Dantewada Campus!
